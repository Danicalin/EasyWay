# EasyWay
## Smart City Companion for Elders
### Target Users:
 Residents aged 60+ that are not comfortable with using complex apps 
### Problem:
Most city and navigation apps nowadays are designed targeting youngsters, making it unfriendly for elders to navigate. This problem overwhelms elders and builds unnecessary stress on them, reducing their independence. Many elders suffer from problems such as forgetting appointments, memory issues, slow typing, confused by a variety of options, unable to compare, ect, making them isolated from technology and develop anxiety when using tech.
